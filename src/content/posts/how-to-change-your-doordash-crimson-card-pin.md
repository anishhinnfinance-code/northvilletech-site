## What Is DoorDash Crimson?

The big difference from DasherDirect is that **Crimson is fully integrated into the Dasher app** — no separate app needed. Everything from checking your balance to managing your card lives inside the same app you already use to dash.

- **No overdraft fees** and no minimum balance requirements
- **Your own account and routing number** — great for direct deposit from other jobs

## How to Set Your DoorDash Crimson PIN

Your PIN is created when you first activate your Crimson card. Here's how the setup works:

**Step 1: Open the Dasher app**
Make sure you're logged into the Dasher app on your smartphone.

**Step 2: Go to the Earnings tab**
Tap the **Earnings** tab at the bottom of the screen.

**Step 3: Access DoorDash Crimson**
Tap the **DoorDash Crimson** entry point. This takes you into your full Crimson banking experience.

**Step 4: Activate your card**
If you haven't activated your card yet, follow the on-screen activation steps. You'll be prompted to create a 4-digit PIN as part of this process.

**Step 5: Choose and confirm your PIN**
Enter your desired PIN and confirm it. Make sure it's something you'll remember — but avoid obvious combinations like 1234 or your birth year.

> **Security tip:** Never write your PIN on or near your card, and never share it with anyone. When entering your PIN at an ATM or store terminal, shield the keypad so others can't see.

## How to Change Your DoorDash Crimson PIN

Need to update your PIN after activation? Here's how to do it through the Dasher app:

**Step 1: Open the Dasher app** and tap the **Earnings** tab.

**Step 2: Tap DoorDash Crimson** to open your Crimson banking dashboard.

**Step 3: Navigate to Card Management**
Look for a **Card** or **Manage Card** section within your Crimson dashboard.

**Step 4: Select "Change PIN"**
Tap the option to change your PIN, then follow the on-screen prompts to enter your current PIN and set a new one.

**Step 5: Confirm your new PIN**
Enter your new PIN twice to confirm and save it.

If you don't see a PIN change option in the app, contact **DoorDash Crimson Support directly** — they can walk you through the process or reset your PIN for you:

## Troubleshooting: Common PIN Issues

**Forgotten your PIN?**
Contact Crimson support at 1-855-973-1040. They can verify your identity and help you reset it. Do not attempt to guess your PIN repeatedly — too many incorrect attempts can temporarily lock your card.

**PIN not working at an ATM?**
Make sure you're using a MoneyPass® network ATM for fee-free withdrawals. If your PIN is declined, try a different ATM and contact support if the issue continues.

**Card locked after too many attempts?**
Call Crimson support immediately at 1-855-973-1040 to unlock your card. Have your Dasher account information ready to verify your identity.

**App not loading your Crimson dashboard?**
Try force-closing and reopening the Dasher app. If the issue persists, check for app updates in the App Store or Google Play, then try again.

**Crimson not showing in your Earnings tab?**
Make sure you've completed the Crimson enrollment process. If you previously had DasherDirect, your account should have transitioned automatically — but if it didn't appear, contact support to ensure your enrollment is complete.
