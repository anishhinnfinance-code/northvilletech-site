## Step 3: Review the list and decide what to cancel

Go through each subscription and mark it as one of three things:

- **Keep** — services you use and want to keep
- **Cancel** — things you want gone

## How to handle Apple and Google Play subscriptions

If a subscription appears in Rocket Money but was purchased through the App Store or Google Play, you need to cancel it at the source:

**On iPhone/iPad:**
1. Open **Settings** → tap your name → **Subscriptions**
2. Find the subscription and tap **Cancel Subscription**

**On Android:**
1. Open the **Google Play Store** → tap your profile icon → **Payments & subscriptions** → **Subscriptions**
2. Find the subscription and tap **Cancel subscription**

Rocket Money will still show these in your list, but cancellation goes through Apple or Google — not Rocket Money.

## After canceling: confirm it worked

A few days after canceling, check back in Rocket Money. The subscription should no longer appear as an active recurring charge in your transaction history.

If a charge still hits after cancellation, contact the provider directly and reference your cancellation confirmation. Rocket Money keeps a record of cancellations it handled on your behalf, which can be useful if you need to dispute a charge.
